Courses We Offer

The road to your degree may be tricky, but we have just the thing for you!
These are some of the courses we offer to prepare you for University!

Foundation Certificate

If you don’t meet the entry requirements to go to a UK university directly, degree preparation courses like a Foundation Certificate can help you reach the level needed to start a bachelor’s degree. You’re guaranteed a place at university when you pass your course at the required level with good attendance.

Pre-Masters

If you don’t quite meet the entry requirements to start a postgraduate degree at a UK university directly, degree preparation courses like a Pre-Master’s can help you reach the level you need to be at. You’re guaranteed a place at university when you pass your course at the required level with good attendance.
